Math

Hi,

My goal here at Ultimate Rankings is to end the "Who's The G.O.A.T." debate once and for all, and put minds of those still on the fence at ease.

The idea was to take all the statistics from all teams and players, from all seasons, figure out which stats matter how much in a team's success, and use the formula to determine who's actually, at least mathematically, the Best Basketball Player Ever.

And I did. If you'd like to know exactly how the weight of each statistic was calculated, and how the player's are ranked, please go to the "Math" section.

But, since half the sport is being able to reach higher than your oponent, you might think that it's not fair to judge all the athletes on the same criteria, whether they're 1.9m, or 2.2m tall. And you'd be right.

So I thought that I should prepare a seperate set of rankings, ones taking into account everyone's height.

And I did. Again, if you're into Math, go to the section by that same name.
